Accessibility

Accessibility is incredibly important!

Do you want:

  • everyone to have a better user experience?
  • to have more customers than your competition?
  • governemnt contracts?
  • to go international with your business?
  • to protect yourself from lawsuits?

If yes, to any of these, then you want your website to be accessible.

Resources

  • W3C WCAG standards
  • Use Chrome Developer Tool Lighthouse

Required

  1. consider contrast - make sure very different colors, contrast checkers are very helpful
  2. h1 required
  3. alt tags for images
  4. links should have an indicator
  5. imgs should show proper cursor
  6. no super small text
  7. tabs should work
